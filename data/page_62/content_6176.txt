  <span id="freeText5947342161336864333" style="display:none">The Dionysus Connection by Liz Cowan is such an exciting read that sometimes you have to catch your breath just to keep up. Murder, mystery, mayhem, and a little romance add to the heady mix that is this book. Dani and Max Fabiani really seem to dislike each other, but at the same time are wildly attracted to one another. Add in the element of trying to solve a murder together, and you have a scintillating story bound to keep you entertained until the very end. Dani is a strong and believable character, and is well drawn. The action pulls you into the believable plot and makes for a fun and satisfying read. Plenty of mystery and intrigue keep the action going throughout this book and make it definitely worth your while to read. A gritty yet realistic setting only adds to the depth and feel of this book.
