I have to agree with the first reviewer - I found this novel totally satisfying! <br /><br />Song Bird has complex characters who it's hard to believe aren't real people somewhere. I loved them all! I was torn between the men and had been since I read the first novel - that is 'In and Out of Step'. I grew as Mavis/Nikki did. While you can read Song Bird as a one off novel, you really benefit from doing the whole journey with her and with Cassie, Kate, and Gary.<br /><br />Reading Song Bird, I got to experience real life in a world very different from my own in all its drama, humour, and adventure. I hadn't thought before about the dilemma a mega female rock star faces when it comes to matters of the heart. It is so real.<br /><br />This author writes beautifully and skilfully. Her style enhanced my reading experience. <br /><br />I rate the Keimera series as the best one I've read in a long while. They deal with something deeper than the literal plot line and for that reason I think the novels will push buttons in some people. <br /><br />I love the symbolism in all three covers. If you enjoy quality story telling and character focused novels, this one is for you. <br /><br /> <br /><br /></span>
