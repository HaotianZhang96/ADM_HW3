  <span id="freeText10307645220990349585" style="display:none">I ordered this book because I am an Author and a stay at home mother. I am looking for new ways to make money from home, successfully so that I can continue to stay at home my children. I believe this book is really going to help me to do that. It is well written and is full of easy to follow steps, tactics, and strategies. I highly recommend this book to everyone that wants to try freelancing, wants to get out of that 9-5 job, stay at home moms or dads, or is just looking to make some extra money on the side!<br /><br />It first teaches what a freelancer is and then teaches me how to become one. When you are a freelancer, you work for yourself or you may be paid by someone to do jobs for them, once the job is complete. This book taught me how to look for clients, land more clients, and how to successfully get the job done correctly. That way my clients will be happy and may even refer more people to me. If I have to pay someone else to do a certain job for me and the client, it teaches me ways to be able to ensure the quality of the work is great. I defiantly need to know this so the client gets what they paid for and not something cheap that won’t work properly or breaks easily. It also teaches you how to protect your money if you pay someone to do a job for you and they do not do it correctly. This is a great thing to learn because if this keeps happening, you will be broke before you get a good start and you can lose your clients and credibility. Once I decide what I want to do and become a freelancer, it teaches me affordable ways to market and advertise myself. There is a lot more to learn in this book, but I am not going to give the rest of this book away. I am now going to end this review and start looking for some clients for myself! <br /></span>
