  <span id="freeText4621437708006432565" style="display:none">It all changed for Michal Siwiec when the baby arrived!<br /><br />Michal hasn't always been an advocate of a healthy lifestyle in terms of physical activity, nutrition and relaxation. As a result, in his early university years his weight peaked at an impressive 103 kg (16 stone), far too much for his stature.<br /><br />The first step for Michal in gaining control of his own life and happiness came with his weight. He implemented a weight-loss programme and successfully shed 36 kg (almost 6 stone) in a year. This empowered Michal and in the following years he embarked on a life-long quest to learn the secrets of a happy life. This led him to investigate the role of nutrition, relaxation and alternative health therapies.<br /><br />In 2006 Michal moved to Ireland to pursue a career in audit and finance. It was all going so well, his career was flourishing and, after getting married, he was happy and content.<br /><br />When Michal discovered he was going to become a father he was delighted, but the reality of bringing up a young baby was something far from his idealistic dreams. The stress and chaos the baby brought meant that his previous simple lifestyle was turned upon its head.<br /><br />All that Michal had learned was about to be stretched to the limit...<br /><br />Take a Deep Breath - this book is the lessons that Michal learned as he tried to claw back some sense of control, whilst maintaining a happy and fulfilled relationship with his wife, his baby and himself.<br /><br />His book Take a Deep Breath presents easily digestible top tips for a healthy and relaxed life. This book is written by a regular guy like all of us, a stressed wage earner, who works 9 to 5 (in fact, longer than that, like most of us), who also struggles with excessive weight and finds it challenging to chill out after an intense day at work. The book is free from professional jargon and easy to read. It was created out of author’s passion to share his way of life to help others, based on his own experience, to improve quality of life of regular guys like you in a number of areas like health, fitness, relaxation and general well-being.</span>
