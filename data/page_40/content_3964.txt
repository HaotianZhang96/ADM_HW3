I don't normally read books like this. I think the last time I read a book about an 11 year old was when I read Harry Potter. Now I've seen this book compared to Harry Potter and I don't really get why. Sure it has wizards and magic but that doesn't mean it has to be compared to Harry Potter. While reading about the Serpent's Eye I actually thought it reminded me of this movie I saw a few years ago that had this ring that everyone was after. I don't remember what the movie was called though.<br /><br />Anyway I thought that <i>The Door in the Sky</i> was a cute and fun read. They acted believable and their age which was great. <br /><br />The book was fairly original too. I don't think I've ever read a book that had stars that were alive. I don't know maybe I missed that part but I just didn't really see a motive for her to want the Serpent's Eye.<br /><br />Other than that the book was pretty good and I really enjoyed reading it.<br /><br /><i>*I received this book from the author.
